## Code of Conduct

We want PullFund to be a welcoming, harassment-free experience for everyone.

### Be respectful

- Be kind and constructive in issues, PRs, and reviews.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions.
- Give credit and be patient with new contributors.

### No harassment

Harassment, discrimination, or hateful conduct is not tolerated.

### Moderation

Project maintainers may remove content and/or restrict participation if it violates this policy.
